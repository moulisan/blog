Reuse Vegetable Plastic Trays

If you buy vegetables/sprouts from Town Essentials or Bigbasket (or even cut vegetables from Namdhari's in Bangalore), you get the vegetables in these light weight plastic trays (see below). For a long time, my wife was wondering what to do with these. This post is dedicated to her lastest reuse idea. Step 1: Take the veggies out. Clean the tray. Step 2: Line the insides with news paper cut to size. Step 3: You can now use this tray to keep Oil containers. This prevents them from soiling your counter top, and also makes sure they dont slip and fall etc. Voila. These plastic trays now have a good use.
