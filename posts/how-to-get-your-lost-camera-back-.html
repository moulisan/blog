How to get your (lost) camera back !

Digital photography school has a cool way ( as suggested by Andrew McDonald) on how to get your camera back, if you had misplaced it, lost it, or just got plain stolen. Put in a series of pictures in it, and give your contacts on how to return it. The more genuine/funny you make it, the larger the chances you may get it back.
