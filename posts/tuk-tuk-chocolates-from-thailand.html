Tuk-tuk chocolates from Thailand

My bro-in-law has just come back from down-undah (the way I always refer to the aussie land). He had a stop-over at Thailand, and he bought these chocolates there. Tuk-tuk is how the thai refer to our dear auto-rickshaw. Wow. Neat idea eh ! (and yes, I had already eaten one auto before the thought struck me that, I could post this !).
