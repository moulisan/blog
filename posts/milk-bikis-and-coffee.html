Milk bikis and Coffee

There is something magical about the taste of Milk bikis dipped in Coffee and eaten. Hmm. The otherwise drap taste of the biscuit becomes brilliantly better. Biting into the succulent biscuit is just nirvana. Ahem, let me stop before I sink more into soliloqui. PS: The same taste is there with Milk Bikis dipped in tea as well - but can be rated a close second. Coffee beats the tea in taste.
