Winston Churchill at his best !

I was tag surfing on wordpress.com when I found this. Amazing. I have heard that both Winston Churchill and George Bernard Shaw were known for their wit, but this one tops them all.

Lady Astor: “Winston, if you were my husband, I should flavour your coffee with poison.”

Churchill: “Madam, if I were your husband, I should drink it.”
