Make XP have a OS X feel

  1. Grab the separator bar to the left of your quick launch bar and pull out of the task bar bar
  2. Right click on the quick launch bar and click view-large icons
  3. Center the quick launch bar
  4. Grab the task bar and lift it to the top of the screen

Viola !
